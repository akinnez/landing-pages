Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Veriphye. These terms govern your relationship with veriphye.com, and we encourage you to read them carefully.

1. What is this app and how does it work?

Our app helps users track their devices in real-time, verify device authenticity, and ensure a safer marketplace for buying and selling devices.

2. Who can use this app?

The app is designed for individuals, merchants, and anyone looking to secure or verify their devices.

3. Is this app free to use?

We offer a free plan with basic features and premium plans with advanced functionality for individuals and businesses.

4. What types of devices can I track?

You can track any device with a unique identifier, including smartphones, laptops, tablets, and more.

5. Does this app work internationally?

Yes, our app provides tracking and verification services globally, as long as the device is registered and has an active connection.

6. How does the real-time tracking feature work?

By linking your device's unique ID or GPS functionality, the app provides live location updates whenever the device is online.

7. Can I track multiple devices?

Yes, you can track multiple devices. The number depends on your subscription plan.

8. What happens if my device is offline?

The app stores the last known location of your device and will update its position once it reconnects.

9. Can I receive alerts if my device moves unexpectedly?

Absolutely! You can set up alerts for unauthorized movement or activity.

10. What should I do if my device is stolen?

Report it immediately through the app. It will alert nearby users and merchants to help locate or identify the stolen device.

11. How does the device verification feature work?

Enter or scan the device’s unique ID to check its history, ownership, and whether it’s flagged as stolen.

12. Is the verification process reliable?

Yes, our database is updated in real-time to ensure accurate and trustworthy results.

13. How can merchants benefit from this app?

Merchants can authenticate devices, build customer trust, and manage their inventory with advanced tools.

14. Can I verify a device before purchasing it from a seller?

Yes, you can use the verification tool to check a device’s status and history before buying.

15. How secure is my data?

We prioritize your security. All data is encrypted and stored in compliance with industry standards to protect your privacy. Check privacy policies for more details.
